How Does CrowdBite Work?

1. Join Network

Sign-up for free by completing an online profile. Receive exclusive access to deals and additional benefits based upon your profile, investment criteria, and membership status.

2. Select Investments

Browse and evaluate different investments. Skillfully analyze deals with the help of the “crowd” and draw conclusions about the property’s location, risk, and return projections that YOU are comfortable with.

3. Transact Online

Execute all parts of the transaction securely right on our website, including signing legal documents and transferring funds. Transferred funds are held in escrow until 100% of the funding goal is reached.

4. Manage Portfolio

Through your investor dashboard, you will be able to track the status of your investment, download tax documents, and receive distributions.
